Welcome to Planet Park, {{ request.user }}!

Planet Park is a web application that allows users to explore the great variety of National Parks across the United States, and hopefully discover some new places to visit. The site consists of two main components: park discovery and park reviews.

The Discovery page allows users to find new parks using a variety of filters, such as location and available activities. Directly from this page users can navigate to National Park specific information pages with detailed information about each park, including important alerts, current weather, and suggestions for specific activities across the park!

The Reviews page allows users to share and rate their experiences with the rest of the site to inspire others to go as well! You can explore the different reviews posted by users across the site or choose to write a review of your own. You can even attach your own photos of your experience to the review!
